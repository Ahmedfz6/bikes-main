Our programs

We, the members of Tunisian Bikers, are pleased to share our riding passion with you through the organization of a seven-day riding journey to explore some of the top Tunisian attractions.

the first day

El Mouradi Hotel Hammamet will be our meeting place on the first day. We will start the journey by heading to the northern half of Tunisia to discover a must-see Tunisian attraction. A one-hour ride from Hammamet to Zaghouan will be enjoyable. Once we get there, a variety of spots are worth exploring. After spending a day out discovering the governorate, we will go back to Hammamet where to spend a cozy evening.

Schedules

the second day

Our second destination will be to the north coast of Cap Bon. The distance from Hammamet to Korbous is a one-hour ride to discover the small but unique neighboring city famous for its gushing hot springs and distinguished architecture. We will spend an unforgettable day sightseeing, wandering, and exploring the place before going back to Hammamet.

Schedules

the third day

The next ride will be the farthest since we will be heading south. So, the third day will be our day to come up for air. Thus, it is up to you to choose how to spend it.

Schedules

the fourth day

Tozeur will be our next destination on the fourth day, and after a six-hour ride, we will reach the Land of Palm. We will be exploring some Tunisian attractions on the way. Our visit to the southwest will be mesmerizing and impressive. El Mouradi Tozeur is the accommodation where we will spend the rest of our journey.

Schedules

the Fifth day

Once in Tozeur, there are no excuses to miss the splendid beauty of the city. There are various attractions and spots to see and explore. That's why we will start our journey by exploring the unique attic city and many other places.

Schedules

the sixth day

On the sixth day, the desert is our destination. The city of Douz hence is our getaway to the Sahara. The Star Wars sets, the Dunes, and the jeep tours in the depths of the desert are some of the most unforgettable experiences to have before going back home.
